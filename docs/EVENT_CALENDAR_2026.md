## NOTES
- **GastroCanarias** is the biggest gastronomy event in Canarias. ~80 exhibitors, 205 stands, 14 cooking championships. The exit sampling strategy (hitting people as they LEAVE at 13:00 and 18:00) is higher ROI than paying for a booth.
- **Corpus Christi La Orotava** (June 11–15) has the highest pedestrian density in Q2 — tens of thousands walk the flower carpets.
- **Noche de San Juan** (June 23) is the biggest beach party night of the year. Mobile sampling with backpacks beats booth setup.
