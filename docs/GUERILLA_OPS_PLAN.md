## PHASE 0: LEGAL SHORT-CIRCUIT (Do This Weekend)

You don't need a €50k kitchen. You need a **Registro Sanitario number on your label**.

| Step | Action | Cost | Time |
|------|--------|------|------|
| 1 | Get Carnet de Manipulador de Alimentos (online) | €20 | 1 hour |
| 2 | Find a licensed Pastelería/Quesería in Santa Cruz/La Laguna | €100-200/mo | This weekend |
| 3 | Print labels with THEIR license number + your brand + ingredients + expiry | €50 | 2 days |
| 4 | Buy isothermal backpacks + ice packs for mobile sampling | €80 | 2 days |
| 5 | Register as Autónomo (needed for grants later, not for day-one sales) | €0 | 1 day online |

**The Ghost Kitchen Deal:** Walk into any local bakery or cheese shop. "I make artisanal Greek yogurt. I'll pay you €150/month to use your counter for 4 hours on Sunday night to strain and jar." You are now legally producing under their RGSEAA. Their license number goes on your jar.

## THE SAMPLE-TO-SALE TECH LOOP

Every sample cup/jar MUST have a QR code. The QR goes to your `/yogurt` landing page.

**Landing Page Flow:**
1. Scan QR → See a sexy product photo + "4 jars for €10, delivered tomorrow"
2. Drop WhatsApp number + Name
3. You (Engineer) get a notification
4. Send Stripe Payment Link via WhatsApp Business
5. Close the sale in < 5 minutes

**The Reels Loop:**
- Film every sampling session.
- Post 3 Reels/TikToks per day showing the product being handed to real people.
- Caption: "Hoy estamos en [Location]. Ven a probarlo."
- Every reel ends with "Link en bio para pedir."

## MATH

- 1 liter of milk = ~600g strained yogurt
- Cost per liter: ~€1.20 (if buying from local dairy)
- 1 jar (450g) sells for €5–7
- Sample cups (50ml) cost ~€0.15 each including yogurt
- Target: Give 100 samples → capture 30 leads → close 10 sales → €50 revenue per 100 samples
- At scale: 500 samples/weekend = €250 revenue, but the real money is in the **subscription box**.

**Subscription is the product.** Sampling is just customer acquisition.
